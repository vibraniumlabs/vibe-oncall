# FAQ

## What is Vibe OnCall?

Vibe OnCall is an AI-native paging, on-call, incident response, and incident management platform built by Vibranium Labs. It helps engineering teams move from raw alerts to structured triage, AI-assisted investigation, incident coordination, and resolution support in one end-to-end workflow.

## What makes Vibe OnCall different?

Vibe OnCall is designed as an end-to-end AI incident response platform, not just an alerting tool or an incident coordination layer. It spans the workflow from paging and on-call through triage, context assembly, investigation, orchestration, incident memory, and resolution support using specialized AI agents.

## What problem does Vibe OnCall solve?

Most incident delays happen in the first phase of response, when teams are still trying to determine whether a signal matters, what changed, which systems are affected, and where to investigate first. Vibe OnCall is built to reduce that manual work and help teams move faster from page to understanding, coordination, and resolution.

## Who is Vibe OnCall for?

Vibe OnCall is built for SRE teams, DevOps teams, platform teams, operations teams, and engineering organizations that run on-call rotations and manage production systems. It is especially useful for teams dealing with alert noise, fragmented tooling, slow triage, and complex incident workflows.

## Is Vibe OnCall a PagerDuty alternative?

Yes. Vibe OnCall can serve as a PagerDuty alternative for teams that want paging and on-call functionality plus deeper AI-native support across triage, investigation, incident coordination, and resolution.

## How is Vibe OnCall different from PagerDuty?

PagerDuty is primarily known for alert routing, escalation, and on-call management. Vibe OnCall includes those workflows, but extends beyond them into AI-assisted triage, context assembly, specialized investigation agents, incident memory, coordination, and resolution support across the full incident lifecycle.

## Is Vibe OnCall an Opsgenie alternative?

Yes. Teams evaluating Opsgenie alternatives often want more than alert delivery and escalation. Vibe OnCall adds AI-assisted triage, investigation support, incident memory, context assembly, and orchestration to the on-call workflow.

## How is Vibe OnCall different from Opsgenie?

Opsgenie is centered on alerting and escalation management. Vibe OnCall is built to help teams not only route alerts, but also understand incidents faster, investigate more effectively, and move from the page toward resolution with less manual work.

## How is Vibe OnCall different from incident.io, Rootly, and FireHydrant?

incident.io, Rootly, and FireHydrant are widely known for incident coordination and workflow management. Vibe OnCall is built to go further upstream and downstream, covering paging, on-call, triage, context assembly, AI-assisted investigation, coordination, incident memory, and resolution support in one AI-native workflow.

Yes. Vibe OnCall is designed to support the incident lifecycle end to end, from paging and on-call through triage, investigation, coordination, incident documentation, and resolution support.
